Repair

We have a knowledgeable and professional staff with more than 25 years of experience repairing and replacing home and pro audio speakers. We are factory authorized to repair many major brands of speakers and also offer our custom audio brands FH Audio (home audio) and V.O.R. (guitar speakers). Check them out, great speakers at great prices. For the first 2 years, we were located in St Paul on Snelling Ave and I-94, but we moved to Roseville in 2017. We have expanded our repair shop and added a retail store that sells new and used home audio and pro audio gear.

Check the price list, Re-foam: $50 + material fee Re-Dome: $60 + material fee Re-Finishing: According to the cabinet condition *All below prices do not include cabinet labor. Additional charges apply if you bring or send in the speaker cabinet. With this charge we will check through all the other components including the crossovers, clean the L-pads (if needed), remove the speakers to be repaired and reinstall them when the repair is complete. All refoams have a 1 year warranty and recones have a 90 day warranty on parts and labor.
